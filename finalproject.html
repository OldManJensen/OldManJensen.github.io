Flutterball! My final Project!

Flutterball is a game I made where you navigate a ball up and down the screen through obstacles. Pass through a certain amount of obstacle to gain enough points to advance to the next level! If you hit an obstacle, you lose a point : (. Overall there are three levels in the game, each equipped with a skip button in case you are frustrated! Depending on how many levels you skip determines the award you get at the end of the game. For instructions on how to play, there is a help menu that gives you every detail you need! Good luck!
